How to play

Build and Bet: Use randomly dealt numbers from 1-10 and arithmetic operation cards (plus, minus, divide, multiply, square root) to construct equations that are close to 1 or 20. You'll wager chips depending on how confident you feel about your equation.

Two Ways to Win a Hand: Bet on low (closest to 1) or high (closest to 20) and have the equation that is closest to that value. You may even be able to win a hand on a bluff. Feeling lucky? Swing bet on both by making two equations with the same cards, one close to 1 and another close to 20.
